Culture
  • Language: Turkey's official language is Turkish and is ofcourse widely spoken in turkey.
  • Religion: Islam is the largest religion in Turkey, 99% of the population being Muslim.
  • Cuisine: Mostly eaten cuisines in Turkey are kebabs, kofte, mezes and dough-based desserts such as baklava, şöbiyet, kadayif, katmer and künefe.
  • Celebration: Hidirellez, Cappadocia Balloon Festival, Mesir Paste Festival, Istanbul Tulip Festival, Anniversary of the Anzac campaign and may more.
  • Traditional Clothes: Traditional Turkish men's clothing includes loose trousers (Shalvar), a long-sleeved shirt (Gömlek), a vest (Yelek), and a long robe (Entari) or a short jacket (Cepken). Women wear a vest (Yelek), a long-sleeved shirt (Gömlek), a flowing dress (Entari), loose trousers (Şalvar), and a wide-sleeved robe (Kaftan) on special occasions.
Costs
It is better to book in advance to get lower prices.
  • Flight Tickets: The least expensive round trip flights for LHR to Istanbul usually costs between £155-£245.
  • Hotel: Hotels can cost from £22 to around £140 per night.
  • Food: Food can cost around £25 per day.

Overall you would need around £47 to £165 per day.

Places to Visit
  • Galata Tower: A medieval tower offering panoramic views of Istanbul's skyline and the Bosphorus.
  • Blue Mosque: Known for its stunning blue tiles and six minarets, the Blue Mosque is a significant Islamic landmark.
  • Bosphorus: Take a boat trip along the Bosphorus Strait to enjoy panoramic views of the city, bridges, and historical landmarks.
  • Hagia Sophia: Originally built as a cathedral in the 6th century, it later became an Ottoman mosque and is now a museum. It showcases impressive Byzantine architecture.
  • Topcapi Palace Museum: A historic palace that served as the main residence and administrative headquarters of the Ottoman sultans for almost 400 years.
  • Grand Bazaar: One of the world's oldest and largest covered markets, offering a vast array of shops selling jewelry, textiles, spices, and more.
  • Sulemaniye Mosque: Another significant Ottoman mosque, known for its majestic dome and stunning views of the city.
Best Time to Visit
I would recommend Spring (April to June), giving you less crowd and pleasent weather.
